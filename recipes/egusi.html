Egusi Soup

Description

Egusi soup is a thick, hearty soup made from ground melon seeds and vegetables. Itâ€™s a traditional Nigerian favorite often eaten with pounded yam or fufu.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ground egusi (melon seeds)
  • 1/2 cup palm oil
  • 1/2 pound assorted meat
  • 1 tablespoon ground crayfish
  • 1 teaspoon seasoning powder
  • 2 cups chopped spinach or bitterleaf
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Boil meat with seasoning until tender.
  2. Heat palm oil and stir in ground egusi to form a thick paste.
  3. Add meat stock and simmer until egusi is cooked.
  4. Add meat, vegetables, and crayfish. Stir well and cook for 10 more minutes.
  5. Serve with pounded yam or fufu.
