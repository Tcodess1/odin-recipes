Fried Rice

Description

Nigerian fried rice is a colorful, savory dish usually served at parties. It’s cooked with vegetables, liver or shrimp, and flavored with curry, thyme, and stock.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups parboiled rice
  • 1/2 cup diced liver or shrimp
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, green beans)
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1 teaspoon curry
  • Salt, pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Boil rice in stock until cooked, then drain excess liquid.
  2. Sauté onions, liver/shrimp, and veggies in oil with curry and seasonings.
  3. Add cooked rice and stir-fry together.
  4. Serve hot with chicken or salad.
